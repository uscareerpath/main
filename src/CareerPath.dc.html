Your career moved with you.
Let's help you rebuild it.

CareerPath helps immigrant professionals in the U.S. figure out their next steps with visa-aware guidance, weekly goals that adapt to your pace, real opportunities, and mentors who've walked the same road.

Join the waitlist Volunteer with us

Most professionals who immigrate here watch their careers shift, stall, or restart from scratch — through no fault of their own. CareerPath exists to help you find the next step.

What we do

Four things, working together for your next move.

01

Visa-aware guidance

Advice that knows your status. H-4, L-2, F-1 OPT, TN — guidance that accounts for what you can and can't do right now, not generic career tips.

02

Adaptive weekly goals

Small, doable steps instead of an overwhelming to-do list. A weekly plan that adjusts to your field, your timeline, and how much time you actually have.

03

Opportunity discovery

Roles, fellowships, and openings matched to where you actually are — including ones that fit your work authorization, not where the system assumes you should be.

04

Real human connection

Mentors who've walked it, peers who get it, and volunteering that rebuilds your network and your résumé at the same time.

How it will work

Three steps, at your own pace.

1

Tell us your situation

Your visa, your field, and where you'd like to go. We start from your real circumstances — not a generic template.

2

Get a weekly plan

A few doable steps each week that adapt to your pace and timeline — with the visa context built in.

3

Move forward with people

Mentors, peers, and matched opportunities help you take the next step — and rebuild your network as you go.

Who we focus on first

Built especially for dependent visa holders.

If you're here on an H-4, L-2, F-2, J-2, TD, or O-3, your career often goes on hold while someone else's moves forward. You tend to get the least support and face the most uncertainty. We're building for you first.
